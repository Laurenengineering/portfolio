Aluminum Light Saber Hilt

Project Overview

I designed a custom light saber hilt in Solidworks CAD software. I then machined the hilt out of aluminum using a metal lathe and mill.

Technologies: Python, Drone Design, Machine Learning, CAD

Additional Details

Non-pictured Steps

  • Completed safety training and testing on metal lathe and mill.
  • After machining, I deburred and sandblasted the hilt prior to spray painting the hilt black.
  • After spray painting, I ran the hilt through one last pass on the lathe.
  • I assembled the hilt with electronics and the blade using set screws.

Learning Process

  • Had to ensure that measurements in CAD where standard sizes that matched the tools available on the mill and lathe.
  • Had to consider various design constraints due to machining limitations.
  • Learned the importance of precision, as one small error would throw off the entire design.
  • Practiced more complicated milling operations.
  • Learned more about various ways to set up and orient pieces for successful and safe machining.
